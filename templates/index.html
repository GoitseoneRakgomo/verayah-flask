The Artist
“Every mark is an act of devotion.”

Verayah is a self-taught tattoo artist and fine jewelry maker working at the intersection of ancient symbolism and contemporary luxury. Her work draws from Middle Eastern ornamental traditions — sacred geometry, protective talismans, and botanical motifs — rendered with an editorial precision that feels both timeless and intentional.

Every piece she creates is handmade and singular. Whether tattooed on skin or shaped in gold, each work carries the weight of intention and the lightness of beauty. Based in Los Angeles, available for custom appointments, flash, and consultations worldwide.
